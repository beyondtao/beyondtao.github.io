08.2022

五岳归来不看山，黄山归来不看岳 No mountain is more gorgeous than the Five Great Mountains until you pay a visit to Mount Huang.

Mount Tai is belong to Five Great Mountains. I went climbing Mount Tai this month, it is more than 1500 miters high, at 23 o’clock I began to climb the mountain, it took me five hours to climb to the top.

Standing on the top of MountTai, I feel that my life has just begun. But when I was looking for a job this year, many companies told me that you are too old. But I’m only 31 years old.

So I decided to go to Germany to find a job and start a new life.

04.2022

I communicate here in English, and master better workflow specifications. I send emails to colleagues to confirm work matters, and submit pull requests to colleagues, with colleagues review the code.

Better grash the use of Git. git reset git revert git rebase git discard and so on.

But why did I still leave. Because I think there is no life to work in Beijing. I leave home at 8:00 and return home at 21:00 every working day. When I get home and after washing, it’s time to go to bed. And sometimes I work overtime until 23 or 24 o’clock. And sometimes I need to work in the company on weekends.

In this year and a half, I gained 12 kilograms in weight. I have no time for walking, running, vacation and thinking.

Faceing the reality, I have to start my new job again.

01.2020

Because of the COVID-19, the company is closed. Because everyone is not allowed to go out, I can’t find a new job. Then I decided to do a project with my friends.

In this year, I learned Java and VUE, we did a project. Then use docker to deploy in the Linux server.

Although I didn’t get a dood income, but I learned a lot in this year.

My girlfriend broke up with me. She returned to China. And at this time, I don’t want to continue to apply for the master. So in June, I returned to China, too.

I spent some time at home with my parents, and then went out to travel with friends.
